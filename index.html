Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. Why are some of my keys not lighting up?

If you press a key and it does not turn blue on our tester, your hardware may have a mechanical failure or a "dead switch." This is common in older laptop keyboards or mechanical boards with dust build-up.

2. What is Keyboard Ghosting?

Ghosting is when you press multiple keys at once, and some do not register. Our tool allows you to press up to 10 keys simultaneously to test your keyboard's N-Key Rollover (NKRO) capability.

3. Does this tool record my keystrokes?

No. Privacy is our priority. This tool runs entirely on JavaScript (client-side), meaning no data is sent to our servers. It is safe for testing passwords or sensitive typing.

How to Repair a Non-Working Key

If our tester identified a dead key, try these steps: 1. Use compressed air to clean under the keycap. 2. Update your keyboard drivers in Device Manager. 3. Try the keyboard on a different USB port.
